35 Billion Probiotic Shelf Stable 8 Strains - 60 Vegetarian Capsules Pills

LuckyVitamin's high-quality 35 Billion Probiotic is made with 8 shelf-stable strains. Probiotic bacteria, like Lactobacillus acidophilus and Bifidobacterium bifidum, has a positive effect on your intestinal microflora balance by minimizing the presence of harmful bacteria, enhancing proper digestion, and strengthening your immune system's resistance to infection. The probiotic strains featured in Lucky Vitamin's 35 Billion Probiotic strains are stabilized using a patented poly-matrix system that provides shelf stability at normal room temperature - no refrigeration required. Each Bio-Enhanced, Acid Resistant Strain (BEARS) has been specifically designed to survive stomach acid secretions during digestion. BEARS eliminates the need for enteric coating by preventing the destruction of viable cells that occurs when weaker strains are exposed to an acid environment. Our ultra high probiotic capsules contain a minimum of 35 billion tary blend supports and nurtures good intestinal flora for a healthy digestive system. LuckyVitamin is proud to offer 35 Billion Probiotic that is formulated without salt, dairy, yeast, wheat, corn.
